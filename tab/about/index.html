About

February 11, 2018

Incremental Reader. Software Engineer at X company. Creator of a few libraries, tools, and scripts. Building everyday.


I didn’t start this journey always knowing what I loved to do, I have just slowly started to find it. A long time ago, I remember I talked to a friendly neighbor and asked him how he knew what he wanted to do when he grew up. He laughed and told me he still didn’t know. I didn’t quite understand what that meant at the time. But now that I have been able to learn many things, live in many places, and work a few different jobs, I kind of understand.
The other day, one of my coworkers asked how I knew what I wanted to build and what I wanted to do. I told him I didn’t and remembered my neighbor.

This blog is a collection of all I’ve been able to find out while exploring my own small segment of the world. I’ll try to find out how I can contribute the most value and write it down here.

Have any questions about my work, learning Chinese, or anything? Feel free to ask me here.
